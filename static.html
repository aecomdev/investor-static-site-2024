Our Strategy

We are a complete consulting firm ideally suited for the opportunities ahead partnering with clients to address their primary challenges against a backdrop of rising project size and complexity.  

We have built an organization to take advantage of the multi-decade secular growth drivers of global investment in infrastructure, sustainability, resilience and energy transition, which are benefiting all of our markets. Building on our technical capabilities, we have expanded our addressable market in program management and advisory to participate across the lifecycle of our clients’ programs. This has resulted in a record win rate on our largest pursuits and has allowed us to capture a greater share of the profit opportunity on a project.
